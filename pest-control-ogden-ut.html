Pest Prevention Tips for Ogden Homeowners

The best pest control is proactive. Here are expert tips specific to Ogden's climate and common pest pressures:

  • Schedule a spring inspection before pest season peaks in April–May.
  • Install weatherstripping and door sweeps in fall to prevent rodents from entering as temperatures drop.
  • Clear gutters and downspouts in spring — clogged gutters create standing water for mosquitoes.
  • Stack firewood at least 20 feet from the home and off the ground to deter rodents and ants.
  • Seal gaps around utility lines, plumbing, and the foundation before winter sets in.
