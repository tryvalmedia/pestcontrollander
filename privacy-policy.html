Privacy Policy

PestControlQuotesNow.com Effective: January 1, 2026 Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how PestControlQuotesNow.com collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when visiting our website or speaking with one of our licensed pest control partners.

Who We Are

PestControlQuotesNow.com is an independent pest control referral service operated by Tryval Media, based in Denver, Colorado. We connect homeowners and renters with licensed, insured pest control professionals across all 50 states.

We are not a pest control company. We do not perform treatments or inspections directly. Any service you may receive is provided by a third-party licensed exterminator. Questions? Contact us at hello@pestcontrolquotesnow.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide, which may include:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Your ZIP code and general location
  • The type of pest you are dealing with
  • Information provided during calls with pest control professionals

We also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and visit timestamps. This is used solely for analytics and improving your experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used to:

  • Connect you with a licensed exterminator in your area
  • Provide you with a free pest control quote
  • Follow up on your inquiry if you requested a callback
  • Improve our website and the services we offer
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

We will never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes unrelated to your pest control inquiry. Any sharing of your information is limited to licensed pest control professionals helping fulfill your service request.

Sharing Your Information

When you request a quote through our website or by calling our number, your information may be shared with:

  • Licensed, insured pest control companies in your area
  • Service providers who assist in operating our website (hosting, analytics)

All parties who receive your information are required to use it only to provide you with pest control services. We may also disclose information if required by law or court order.

TCPA Consent & Communications

By submitting your information or calling our number, you consent to be contacted by PestControlQuotesNow.com and its licensed pest control partners via telephone — including calls using automated dialing systems or prerecorded messages — and via text message and email at the contact information you provided.

This consent is not a condition of purchasing any service. You may opt out at any time by calling us, emailing us, or replying STOP to any text message.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies, including:

  • Session cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Analytics cookies that help us understand how visitors use our site
  • TrustedForm lead verification cookies that document your consent to be contacted

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may be affected.

Data Security

Our website uses SSL encryption (HTTPS) to protect data in transit. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your state, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@pestcontrolquotesnow.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we become aware of such collection, we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance.
